Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here’s how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our website, create an account, or interact with our services. This information includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • Demographic Information: Age, gender, and location.
  • Account and Profile Information: Details you provide when you set up a profile, such as dietary preferences and meal choices.
  • Transaction Information: Details about purchases you make through our website, including billing and payment information.
  • Technical Information: Data about your device and network, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, including pages visited, links clicked, and features used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Operate Our Services: We use your data to manage your account, process transactions, and offer personalized meal planning.
  • To Improve and Personalize Your Experience: We analyze your data to better understand your preferences, helping us enhance our services to meet your needs.
  • To Communicate with You: We use your contact information to send you updates, notifications, and promotional materials. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time.
  • To Maintain Security and Prevent Fraud: We monitor and analyze user activity to detect and prevent unauthorized access or activity that could harm our users or our service.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use your information as necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information under the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party vendors who help us provide and improve our services. These providers are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by authorities, such as a court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred to the new entity as part of the transaction.

Your Rights and Choices

You have specific rights regarding your personal information. Depending on your location, these rights may include:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can ask us to correct or update it.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request that we delete your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to limit the processing of your personal data in specific cases.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format to be transferred to another service provider.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data in certain situations, such as direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@mealme.com.

Security Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. These measures include:

  • Data Encryption: We use encryption protocols to secure data transmitted to and from our website.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and they are required to adhere to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Data Retention: We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.
  • Regular Security Audits: We perform regular security assessments to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

Despite these efforts, please be aware that no security measures are foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
